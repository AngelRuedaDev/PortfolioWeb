About me

Hello! I’m Angel Rueda, and I made this website to introduce myself.


I was born in Girona, a city in Catalonia, and grew up in the nearby village of Sant Gregori. Since childhood, I’ve been surrounded by computers thanks to my father, who works in IT. That early exposure sparked a curiosity in me — I wanted to understand how these machines worked. Eventually, that curiosity turned into a passion for software development.


After high school, I completed a Education and Training program (CFGM) in Microcomputer Systems and Networks, where I gained a strong foundation in hardware and operating systems. I then pursued a Certificate of Higher Education (CFGS) in Multiplatform Application Development, which introduced me to both mobile and desktop development and helped me discover my love for programming


I recently graduated with a Bachelor's Degree in Techniques for Software Application Development from UOC (Universitat Oberta de Catalunya). Throughout my academic journey and personal projects, I’ve grown particularly fond of mobile development and backend development, as I enjoy building applications that are both intuitive and robust


I’m currently looking for new professional opportunities where I can continue to grow, contribute, and apply everything I’ve learned — always aiming to write clean, maintainable, and efficient code.
